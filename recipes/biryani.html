Biryani Recipe

Description

Biryani is a celebration of all that is great about Indian food the heady aromas, the vibrant colours, the fluffy rice and those addictive curry flavours. Make this Chicken Biryani with your protein of choice or try a vegetable biryani!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Basamti Rice
  • Fried Onion
  • Ghee
  • Saffron

Steps

  1. Place pot with chicken in it onto a stove over medium heat. Cover and cook for 5 minutes.
  2. Remove lid. Cook for 5 minutes, turning chicken twice.
  3. Top with all the rice. Gently pat down and flatten surface.
  4. Drizzle saffron across rice surface in random pattern, then drizzle over ghee.
  5. Place lid on. Return to stove over medium heat.
  6. Remove from stove, rest with lid on for 10 minutes.
